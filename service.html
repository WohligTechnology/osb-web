Mobile App

An application which enables the people to barter their goods at the click of a button. In the olden ages, barter system was the way to sell and purchase goods, our ancestors considered it to be the best ways in obtaining things that they needed and give away things that they dint need any more to the people who wanted them.

One stop barter gives this old age tradition of barter system a modern twist which enables the moderns to barter goods and get the best of deals with the help of the internet. All one has to do is log in to the application, put up the things which they want to sell and later search for things from among the many users which they want to purchase. This way, they have a variety to choose from just by sitting at one place.
